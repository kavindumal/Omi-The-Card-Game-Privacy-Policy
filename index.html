Omi Game is designed for players of all ages. We do not display or allow content that is inappropriate for children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data being collected, we will delete it immediately.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect user data, but no system is 100% secure. Play responsibly and avoid sharing sensitive data.
